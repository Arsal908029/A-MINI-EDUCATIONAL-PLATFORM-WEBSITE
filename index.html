Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform

What's the cost of the learning modules?

We offer both free and premium modules. Individual courses range from $49-$99, or you can get full access to all courses through our monthly subscription ($29/month) or annual plan ($249/year).

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes, we offer verifiable digital certificates for all completed premium courses. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and added to your resume to showcase your skills to employers.

What kind of support is available?

We provide multiple support channels including community forums, weekly live Q&A sessions, and direct messaging with course instructors for premium members. Our average response time is under 4 hours during business days.

Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We also have a mobile app available for iOS and Android for offline learning.
